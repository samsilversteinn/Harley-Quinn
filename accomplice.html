Harley Quinn & The Joker

Despite being strongly influenced by the Joker, Harleen Quinzel falls in love with him during their therapy sessions and voluntarily joins his team. The two colors of her hair (red and blue) symbolize the bipolar nature of their relationship: a mix of love and hate.
